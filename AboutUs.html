The workshop

A small workshop with a singular focus: create exceptional jewelry without exception.

Every piece is crafted by hand. Gold is recycled. Stones are traceable. Trends don't interest us—craftsmanship does.

We work slowly, deliberately. The same way it's always been done. Because when you take shortcuts, it shows. And we refuse to let that happen.
